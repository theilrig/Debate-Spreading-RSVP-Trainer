Formatting Help
  1. Paste your case into the editor below.
  2. Preferred format: put each tagline on its own line and make the entire tagline bold, including the author/date.
  3. Put the card text on the next line as normal plain text, not bolded.
  4. Repeat that pattern for every card: one bold tagline line, then one or more plain-text card lines.
  5. Contention names: bold and underline the contention header (e.g. Contention 1: Economic Growth). The app will flash it briefly as a section marker and skip straight to the next card — no RSVP.
  6. If bold formatting does not survive paste, format each card as one paragraph: Tagline: card text. The colon tells the app where the tagline ends.
  7. Click Parse after editing, then use Start to read through the case.
